School Resumption Update

Nigeria's Minister of State for Education, Chukwuemeka Nwajiuba, says no date has been fixed yet for full schools' resumption even though the federal government had continued to engage stakeholders.

This has induced mixed feelings amongst the student population. While some students relish, and support the decision of the minister, others abhor it.

Whatever the feelings may be, it is not economically favourable for academic institutions to remain closed.
